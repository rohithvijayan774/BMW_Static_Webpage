50 Years Of BMW M.

BMW M has long stood for all things exhilarating - true sportiness, raw thrill,
and most of all, pure JOY. Now, on the 50ᵗʰ anniversary of M, BMW celebrates
five successful decades of thrilling performance, power-packed upgrades, and
of pushing boundaries. We are grateful to the community of fans, enthusiasts,
and drivers, who have brought to life the ethos of M for the past 50 years. And
to celebrate this momentous occasion, we’re launching exciting ’50 Years of
M’ editions through the year.

LEGENDARY MOTORSPORT LOGO.

50 years of M sees the return of the iconic BMW Motorsport emblem. BMW M
is putting down a marker for those who love tradition. The logo with the
staggered semicircles in blue, purple and red was first used on race cars of
BMW Motorsport GmbH, founded in 1972.
